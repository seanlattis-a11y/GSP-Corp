The Opportunity / Why Now

U.S. grid and large load customers will require 700–900 GW of additional clean firm capacity by 2050. Target of ~90 GW of geothermal capacity by 2050 via EGS.

  • EGS projects still face significant technical risk across phases
  • Investors and lenders need subject‑matter expertise to mitigate project risk
  • Project developers, EPCs and O&M providers lack EGS‑specific experience
  • Oil & gas expertise translates imperfectly to the EGS domain
  • Market growth will exacerbate the knowledge gap — creating opportunity for GPS
